A Brief Review of CSC3100

  1. JAVA implement of AVL tree

To balance the tree, we shall maintain the depth difference between the two subtrees of one node. Rotate the node when the absolute value of the difference is 2. The rotation is in much the same way as treap or splay, and lead to rebalance of the tree (the node has unique subtree height after rotation).

Although highly balanced, AVL tree is relatively slow for it frequently rotates (?).
